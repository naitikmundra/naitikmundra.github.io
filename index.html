About Cyber Alley

  • Online safety for everyone

    Cyber Alley teaches everyone who uses internet regardless of their background. With Cyber Alley you learn about various threats,scams and frauds that can harm you and your computer directly or indirectly. Teaches you ways you can prevent them and how they function.

  • How we benefit students through their university life

    In this ever changing world where even the smallest aspects of learning and education in colleges and universities have taken digital turn, its important for students to keep themeselves safe as they travel across this realm of online education , cyber alley is all about giving the student that trust and feeling of security, so they can focus solely on their studies and leaving the tension on our hand
